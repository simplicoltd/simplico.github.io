About us

Built on one simple idea

SimpliCo Ltd was founded with a straightforward premise: sell everyday products on Amazon the right way. That means sourcing only from authorised UK wholesalers, maintaining proper records, and never cutting corners on compliance.

Every product listed under the SimpliCo name comes from a legitimate, verified source — properly invoiced and accurately described. Orders are fulfilled through Amazon, so buyers have the same protections and delivery standards they'd expect from any Amazon purchase.

SimpliCo Ltd is a registered company in England and Wales. Contact details are in the section below.

What we sell

We sell everyday essential products — the kind of things people buy regularly, from brands they already know and trust. Our range grows as we find products that meet our sourcing criteria: genuine branded stock, available from authorised UK distributors, at a price that makes sense for buyers on Amazon.

We don't chase trends or sell anything we can't source properly. If it's listed under the SimpliCo name on Amazon, it came from a legitimate UK wholesaler with a proper VAT invoice — that's the only rule we work to.

Our range is still small. We're building it carefully rather than quickly. Visit our Amazon storefront to see what's currently listed.

How to order

Everything ships through Amazon

We don't take orders directly — all our stock is sent to Amazon's warehouse and fulfilled by them. That means the usual Amazon experience: tracked delivery, easy returns, and standard buyer protection on every order.

1

Search for SimpliCo on Amazon UK

Go to amazon.co.uk and search "SimpliCo" to find our current listings.

2

Add to basket and check out as normal

Amazon handles the payment, packing, and dispatch — exactly as you'd expect.

3

Any issues — contact us or Amazon

For product questions, email us directly. For order or delivery issues, Amazon's own support is usually fastest.
